Lowitja O'Donoghue Cultural Centre, ANU

Address: Tangney Rd, Acton ACT 2601 Australia

Conference Dates: December 1-5, 2025

Events: Main conference sessions, workshops, tutorials, keynotes, poster sessions

The Lowitja O'Donoghue Cultural Centre sits on the ANU campus beside Lake Burley Griffin and will host the core AJCAI activities. It offers flexible lecture spaces and breakout rooms for a full week of sessions and networking.

Transportation: Easily accessible via the Kambri Precinct with public transport, taxi drop-off on Tangney Road, and nearby underground parking.

Room 2.02, Marie Reay Teaching Centre

Address: 155 University Ave, Canberra ACT 2601 Australia

Symposium Date: December 1, 2025

DAIRNet's AI Symposium will take place in Room 2.02 of the Marie Reay Teaching Centre in the Kambri Precinct. The modern lecture theatre is a short walk from the Cultural Centre, making it easy to move between AJCAI events.

Nearby: Kambri food outlets, accessible pathways, and the Childers Street bus stop.

Old Parliament House

Address: 18 King George Terrace, Parkes ACT 2600, Australia

Banquet Date: December 4, 2025 (Evening)

Event: AJCAI 2025 Conference Banquet Dinner

The Old Parliament House is a historic and iconic venue in Canberra, serving as Australia's Parliament from 1927 to 1988. Now operating as the Museum of Australian Democracy, it provides an elegant and prestigious setting for the conference banquet.

Features: Historic architecture, beautiful gardens, and a sophisticated atmosphere perfect for networking and celebration.

üö∂ Getting Here

For more details on the precinct, see Kambri and the Kambri Accessibility Guide.

Travel by foot

  • Head to the Kambri Precinct from your accommodation.
  • University Avenue provides an accessible pathway from Childers Street to Kambri.

Travel by public transport

  • You can get to the Kambri Precinct by bus and light rail.
  • The nearest bus stop is on Childers Street (0.2 km from Kambri Precinct).
  • The nearest light rail stop is on Alinga Street Canberra City (1.0 km from the Kambri Precinct).
  • University Avenue provides an accessible pathway from Childers Street to Kambri.

Travel by taxi

  • Ask to be dropped off at the end of Kingsley Street, Acton.
  • You can get an accessible taxi with 13WATS. Accessible taxis should be booked in advance.

Parking

  • Paid parking is available at the Kambri Precinct underground car park (330 spaces, 8 disability bays).
  • The car park entrance is at the corner of Kingsley Street and Tangney Road, Acton.
  • The underground Kambri car park has elevator access to street level.
  • Accessible parking is available on the Acton campus.
