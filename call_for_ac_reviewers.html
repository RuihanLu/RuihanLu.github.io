Call For Area Chairs and Reviewers

AJCAI 2025, the Australasian Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence, is seeking dedicated Area Chairs (ACs) and Reviewers to help build a strong technical program. Join us in contributing to this prestigious AI conference to be held in Canberra, Australia from December 1-5, 2025.

Why Volunteer?

  • Contribute to the advancement of AI research in the Australasian region and beyond
  • Gain visibility in the AI community and expand your professional network
  • Stay at the forefront of cutting-edge AI research
  • Add valuable program committee experience to your CV

Area Chair Responsibilities

As an Area Chair, you will:

  • Manage a pool of reviewers and assign papers based on expertise
  • Guide the review process for a set of papers in your area of expertise
  • Write meta-reviews synthesizing reviewer feedback
  • Participate in program committee meetings to finalize paper decisions
  • Maintain high quality standards for the conference program

Area Chairs should have a PhD in AI or a related field, a strong publication record, and previous reviewing experience. Senior PhD students with significant publication records may be considered for some areas.

Reviewer Responsibilities

As a Reviewer, you will:

  • Provide timely, constructive, and detailed reviews for assigned papers
  • Evaluate papers according to scientific merit, novelty, and relevance
  • Participate in reviewer discussions when needed
  • Adhere to review deadlines and maintain confidentiality

Reviewers should have expertise in AI or related fields and familiarity with academic publishing. PhD students with appropriate knowledge are welcome to apply.

Key Areas of Expertise

We are seeking ACs and Reviewers with expertise in all areas of AI, including but not limited to:

  • Foundation Models and LLMs
  • Artificial Intelligence-Generated Content (AIGC)
  • Computer Vision and 3D Vision
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Explainable AI
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • AI Ethics and Fairness
  • Robotics and Autonomous Systems
  • Quantum Machine Learning
  • Human-AI Interaction
  • AI for Healthcare
  • Multi-agent Systems
  • Knowledge Representation and Reasoning

How to Apply

To apply as an Area Chair or Reviewer, please submit your application through our online form (coming soon) with the following information:

  • Full name and affiliation
  • Position (Professor, Associate Professor, Assistant Professor, Research Scientist, etc.)
  • Email address
  • Link to your Google Scholar profile or personal webpage
  • Top 3 areas of expertise (from the list above or others)
  • Brief description of your reviewing experience (for ACs, please highlight previous AC roles)
  • Role you're applying for (Area Chair, Reviewer, or both)

Important Dates

  • Area Chair Applications Due: March 15, 2025
  • Reviewer Applications Due: April 30, 2025
  • Notification of AC Acceptance: March 31, 2025
  • Notification of Reviewer Acceptance: May 15, 2025

For questions about the AC or reviewer roles, please contact the Program Chairs at program-chairs@ajcai2025.org.

We look forward to your participation in making AJCAI 2025 a success!
