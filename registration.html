Registration Notes

A student is defined as a holder of a student identification card from a recognised tertiary or secondary educational institution or international student card. Students must be currently enrolled in part or full time.

The registration confirmation and tax invoice will be automatically emailed to the delegate upon successful registration. If you have any issues getting the tax invoice, please contact the registration team for assistance.

Cancellation Policy

All cancellations must be made in writing to the registration team (Heming Du, Jackie Rong). If cancellation is not received in writing, the registration will not be cancelled and a refund (if applicable) will not be processed.

  • Cancellations received on or before 16 November 2025 will incur a 50% cancellation fee
  • Cancellations received on or after 16 November 2025 are non-refundable, however transfer of registration to another attendee is acceptable
  • Refunds cannot be made on credit card merchant fees charged at the time of the booking
