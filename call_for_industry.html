Call for Interest: AI in Government and Industry Day

This is a warm invitation to AI practitioners and leaders from government and industry to express their interest to join the AI in Government and Industry Day, a track hosted as part of the 2025 Australasian Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AJCAI). Recognised globally for its impact and breadth, AJCAI is the leading annual forum for AI research and innovation in Australasia.

The Government and Industry Day seeks to convene AI practitioners, government policy experts, and industry leaders from across Australia's public and private sectors. The program shall offer a unique platform to explore cutting-edge advancements, address regulatory and ethical challenges, and share real-world case studies that intersect with AI's transformative potential.

Program Highlights

  • Panels and Spotlight Presentations: Leaders, technology innovators, academics, and research specialists.
  • Parallel to Main Conference: The program will run alongside the main AJCAI conference proceedings, providing opportunities for cross-sector networking and knowledge exchange.

Three Key Themes

  • From Responsible AI Policies to Practical Engineering Solutions
  • AI Adoption and Governance at Scale
  • Public-Private Partnership Models between Academia, Industry and Government

Participation and Registration

Prospective panelists and speakers can express their interest in joining the proceedings of our AI in Government and Industry Day by completing this form. Those invited to join the AI in Government and Industry Day as a panelist or presenter will be required to purchase a day registration, which includes admission to the 2025 AJCAI conference dinner.

We warmly welcome everyone to register to attend the AJCAI 2025 conference, whether you are seeking to present or benefit from the program as an audience participant.

Publication Opportunities

Individuals working in government and industry are also invited to submit papers for inclusion in the main conference proceedings.

Submission Deadlines

  • Expression of Interest for Speakers/Panelists: Please submit your interest, including a title, topic abstract, and biography, using this form by July 31st, 2025.
  • Industry and Government Day Paper Submissions for Main Conference Proceedings: Submissions are due by August 1st, 2025. If you wish to submit a full paper for inclusion in the main proceedings, please review our official EasyChair Submission page for detailed guidelines and submission instructions. Submissions should not exceed 6 pages.
  • Main Conference Full Paper Submission: You may also consider submitting a full 12 page manuscript to the main conference, to be held 1-5th of December 2025. The deadline to do so is July 31st, 2025 on our general Call for Papers.

If you have any questions, please contact chairs.ajcai2025@gmail.com.
