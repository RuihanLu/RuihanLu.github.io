Student Travel Grant

The AJCAI 2025 Student Travel Grant provides free full registration (or reimbursement) to eligible students. This grant is designed to support students who might otherwise face financial barriers to attending the conference.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Student Travel Grant, applicants must meet all of the following criteria:

  • First author of an accepted paper: You must be the first author of a paper accepted at AJCAI 2025
  • Student or recent graduate: You must be currently enrolled as a student (PhD, Master's, or Honours) or have recently graduated
  • From an under-represented group: You must identify with one or more of the following groups:
    • Non-male
    • First Nations
    • Low socio-economic background
    • Rural/regional background

Priority Consideration

Priority will be given to:

  • Students of early-career researchers
  • Students with greater financial need (e.g., those who have to travel further distances and pay for accommodation)

Application Process

To apply for the Student Travel Grant, please complete the application form using the link below. The form will collect information about your eligibility and financial circumstances to help us allocate grants fairly.

Important Notes

  • Masters and Honours students are eligible to apply for this grant
  • Recipients of this grant are also eligible to attend the PhD Forum, where additional awards may be available
  • Applications will be reviewed based on the information provided, including financial status and supervisor's endorsement
  • Grant recipients will receive either free registration or reimbursement for their full registration fee
