Call for Papers: Encore Track

To highlight impactful and cutting-edge research in machine learning and artificial intelligence, we are pleased to announce the Encore Track at our upcoming 2025 conference. This track is designed to showcase significant works that have already appeared at other prestigious venues, while fostering further interactions and discussions among researchers.

Scope of Submissions: Researchers are invited to submit an abstract, title, and author information for recent publications that have been published, are in press, or have been accepted since 1 Jun 2024. Suitable venues include (but are not limited to) top-tier conferences and journals such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ECCV, ICCV, CVPR, ACL, AAAI, JMLR, and IEEE TPAMI.

Presentation Format: Accepted submissions will be presented in the Encore Track, which will run on Tuesday, 1 Dec 2025, as part of the conference week.

Review Process: The review process will begin immediately after submission. Acceptance will be based on the quality and relevance of the work. To enrich discussions at the conference, we seek submissions that not only meet high scientific standards but also contribute to the diversity and breadth of the program.

Submission Requirements: Authors must submit the following via the official Google Form:

  • A PDF of the published paper.
  • The official URL linking to the published version.
  • For accepted but not yet published papers, evidence of acceptance (e.g., acceptance email) in PDF format.

Authors should submit the required information via this Google Form.

Important Dates:

  • Submission Deadline: Continuous until 18 October 2025 (23:59 AEST, UTC+10)
  • Notification of Acceptance: Within 14 days of submission
